Bot Project

The Bot Project is a multi-purpose chatbot I developed to simplify academic Q&As, event updates, and personal productivity for my campus community. Built with JavaScript, Python, and a hint of machine learning, it blends utility with personality—offering reminders, schedules, and even motivational quotes. Future plans include mental health check-ins and astrology-inspired features, keeping its ethereal vibe alive. .
